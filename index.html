manifesto

Decision making

first

The council operates under a democratic process where all members have an equal voice in decision-making.

Major decisions, such as selecting new projects or partnerships, require a supermajority vote to ensure a high level of commitment and excellence.

In the event of a tie, the decision is deferred until the next meeting to allow for further discussion and reflection. The council values thoughtful and deliberate decision-making to ensure the best possible outcomes.

Communication

second

Members are expected to communicate professionally and effectively, with clarity and precision.

Regular and timely communication is essential to ensure that all members are informed and up-to-date on important matters.

Transparency

third

Members must disclose any conflicts of interest or potential conflicts of interest to maintain the council's integrity and reputation.

The council values transparency and openness to ensure that all members have a clear understanding of the council's operations and decisions.

Ethics

fourth

The council holds its members to high ethical standards and expects them to act with integrity.

Members must maintain the confidentiality of sensitive information to protect the council's interests and reputation.

Term limits

fifth

Members who wish to resign may transfer their membership to a newly voted member. The transfer is irreversible and should be made after careful consideration.

The council recommends that resignations be announced to the community in a professional and respectful manner.

Code of conduct

sixth

Members must adhere to the council's code of conduct, which includes guidelines on professionalism, respectful behavior, and ethical standards.

Any violations of the code of conduct will be addressed fairly and promptly by the council's leadership. A vote will be held among council members specifically related to the matter.

Expertise

seventh

Members must maintain a high level of expertise in their respective fields and continuously enhance their knowledge and skills to keep up with the latest developments.

Members are encouraged to share their expertise and knowledge with the rest of the community and help mentor and develop other members. The council believes in building not only great products but also great people.

Accountability

eighth

Members are held accountable for their actions and decisions, in the same manner as we are held accountable for the creation of top-quality blockchain products. Our unwavering dedication to excellence underscores the importance of all members conducting themselves with the highest level of professionalism and integrity.

The Council upholds the standards set forth in our code of conduct and is committed to enforcing them impartially. Should a member deviate from these guidelines, we will not hesitate to initiate corrective action to ensure the welfare of the community as a whole. Our leadership stands ready to provide guidance and support to any member who may require assistance in correcting the course.

Removal of Members

ninth

The council does not tolerate disruptive behavior. If a member is causing issues and acting against the interests of the community, swift action will be taken, and their membership will be revoked.

We take all allegations of misconduct seriously, and a thorough investigation will be conducted to ensure a fair decision is made in the best interest of the community. If a member is found to be at fault, they will be informed of the consequences of their actions.
